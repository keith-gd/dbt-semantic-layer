
Bank of America Institute methodology: The gold standard
The October 2025 Consumer Checkpoint exemplifies rigorous consumer spending analysis. Key methodological elements your debit card program should emulate:

Measurement precision:

Per household spending growth - Isolates households with 5+ transactions monthly to filter out spurious accounts

Seasonally adjusted metrics - 3-month moving averages smooth volatility, annualized rates enable YoY comparisons

Fixed cohort tracking - Baseline from January 2019 enables consistent comparisons even as customer base evolves

Inflation adjustment - Balances tracked in real terms to separate nominal growth from purchasing power

Segmentation approaches:

Income terciles - Lower (bottom 33%), middle (34-66%), higher (top 33%), plus separate top 5% tracking for wealth effects

Generational cohorts - Gen Z, Younger Millennials, Older Millennials, Gen X, Baby Boomers, Traditionalists

Geographic breakdown - Census regions, MSA-level analysis, urban vs. rural patterns

Spending categories - Retail ex-restaurants/gas, discretionary services, non-discretionary services, durables with premium vs. value tiers

Key metrics tracked:

Total credit & debit card spending per household (MoM% and YoY%, seasonally adjusted)

Transaction counts and average transaction values by category

Median checking and savings balances by cohort

After-tax wage and salary growth via direct deposit flows

Credit utilization rates by generation

Share of wallet analysis (discretionary vs. essential spending)

Core debit card program metrics
Customer acquisition & lifecycle:

Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) - Total acquisition costs / New customers; benchmark: $7 (Nubank Latin America), $20-45 (European neobanks)

Card Portfolio Growth Rate - (New cards activated - Cards closed) / Total active cards

Activation Rate - Spend Active: % with 1+ purchase/month; Balance Active: % with balance > $0

Monthly Active Users (MAU) - Unique users with 1+ transaction in 30 days / Total accounts

Daily Active Users (DAU) - Unique users with 1+ transaction in 24 hours; benchmark: 25-35% DAU/MAU for leading neobanks

Products per Customer - Average financial products held; SoFi benchmark: 1.46 products/customer

Churn Rate - Accounts with zero activity for 90+ days / Total accounts

Revenue metrics:

Average Revenue per User (ARPU) - (Interchange + fees + interest) / Active accounts

Interchange Revenue - Basis points on transaction volume; impacted by Durbin Amendment (large banks capped at $0.21 + 0.05%, small banks exempt)

Net Interest Margin (NIM) - (Interest income - Interest expense) / Earning assets; U.S. average: 3%, Nubank: double-digit

Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) - Discounted net revenue over expected customer lifespan; target: LTV/CAC ratio of 3:1 minimum

Transaction metrics:

Total Transaction Volume - Count and dollar amount, segmented by merchant category (MCC codes)

Average Transaction Value (ATV) - Total spend / Transaction count

Transactions per Active User - Total transactions / Monthly active accounts

Authorization vs. Settlement Rates - % of authorized transactions that settle (indicates reversals/declines)

Top-of-Wallet Share - % of total customer card spend on your card vs. competitors

ACH & payments:

Direct Deposit Penetration - % of customers with recurring direct deposit; critical engagement driver (Chime reports 5x higher spend)

ACH Volume - Inbound (credits): payroll, benefits, transfers; Outbound (debits): bill pay, recurring payments

Instant Transfer Usage - % of funding via instant ACH vs. standard 1-3 day

Payroll Deposit Timing - Average days-early delivery vs. traditional banks (competitive differentiator)

Financial health indicators:

Median Account Balance - Tracked by income cohort and generation

Balance Volatility - Standard deviation of daily balances (proxy for financial stability)

Savings Rate - Net deposits / Total inflows (direct deposit + transfers)

Share of Income to Bills - Recurring debits (rent, utilities, loans) / Total inflows

Overdraft Incidents - Frequency of negative balances, average overdraft amount

Risk & fraud:

Fraud Rate - Fraudulent transaction value / Total transaction value; typically measured in basis points

Dispute Rate - Chargebacks filed / Total transactions

15-90 Day NPL Ratio - Accounts 15-90 days past due / Total accounts; Nubank Q4 2024: 4.1%

90+ Day NPL Ratio - Accounts 90+ days delinquent / Total; Nubank Q4 2024: 7.0%

Charge-off Rate - Charged-off balances / Average outstanding balances

Operational efficiency:

Cost to Serve - (Customer service + reload fees + ATM fees + processing) / Active accounts

Customer Service Contact Rate - Support tickets / Active accounts / Month

Net Promoter Score (NPS) - % Promoters - % Detractors; top neobanks: 70-90, traditional banks: 30-50

Metric patterns for behavior analysis
Cohort retention patterns:

Trailing window actives - Users with 1+ transaction in last 7/30/90 days

Continuous activity periods - Consecutive months with activity (detects power users)

Dormancy detection - Days since last transaction, flagged at 30/60/90 day thresholds

Reactivation tracking - Previously dormant accounts that resumed activity

Normalized metrics (per-user basis):

Spend per Active User - Total spend / Monthly active users (removes growth effects)

Transactions per Active Day - Total transactions / DAU (measures engagement intensity)

Revenue per Funded Account - Total revenue / Accounts with >$10 balance (focuses on engaged segment)

Time-based comparisons:

Same-cohort analysis - Compare January 2024 registrants' Month 1 behavior vs. January 2025 registrants' Month 1

Period-over-period growth - MoM%, QoQ%, YoY% with seasonal adjustments

Cohort maturation - How metrics evolve as cohorts age (Month 1 vs Month 6 vs Month 12 behavior)

Segment-based analysis:

High-value user criteria - Top 20% by spend, top 10% by transaction count, top 5% by balance

Persona tagging - Paycheck-to-paycheck (low balance, high transaction frequency), Savers (high balance, low spend), Credit-builders (utilizes credit features)

Merchant category affinity - % of spend in grocery, gas, dining, online shopping by segment
